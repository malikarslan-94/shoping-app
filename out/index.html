How Klinker works?

Our practical step by step procedure makes material procurement simple.

Sign Up
Creating an account on Klinker is easy and simple. You can sign up in a single Go.
Order your materials
Select the materials you require, your delivery address, date and time. We will provide you with quotes from the nearest best value and highest performing suppliers
We'll do the rest
You'll recieve notifications when the suppliers have approved and fulfilled your order. You'll also recieve the delivery dockets and invoices. Most importantly, we'll keep all these important records safe and secure.
